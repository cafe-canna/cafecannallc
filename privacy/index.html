Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026 (placeholder). This policy explains what limited information Cafe Canna's website collects today, and what will apply as new features are added.

1. Overview

Cafe Canna LLC ("Cafe Canna," "we," "us," or "our") operates this website to share information about our upcoming cannabis retail brand and cafe in Virginia. This site is currently informational and pre-launch: it does not process orders, payments, or sell any product. This Privacy Policy describes what information the site collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

2. Information We Collect

At this stage, the site collects only the following:

  • Age verification status. A cookie confirming you acknowledged our 21+ age gate (see Section 3).
  • Waitlist sign-up information. If you use the "Join the Waitlist" form, we collect the email address (and optional first name) you provide.
  • Contact form information. If you use the contact form on our Location page, we collect the name, email address, and message you submit.

We do not currently collect payment information, government identification, or precise location data through this website.

3. Cookies

This site uses one functional cookie:

  • age_verified — set only after you confirm you are 21 or older on our age verification screen. It is a session cookie, meaning it is automatically deleted when you close your browser, and it exists solely to avoid re-asking the age question on every page during the same visit. It does not identify you personally and is not used for tracking or advertising.

We do not currently use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies. Placeholder: if we add an analytics tool (such as a privacy-focused, cookie-based or cookieless analytics service) in the future, this section will be updated to name the provider, describe what it collects, and explain your opt-out options before it is enabled.

4. Waitlist & Contact Form Data

Placeholder — to be finalized before any email marketing begins. If you join our waitlist, we intend to use your email address only to send updates about our launch timeline, licensing status, and opening details. We do not currently use a third-party email marketing platform; once one is selected, this policy will be updated to name the provider and describe how you can unsubscribe. Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your inquiry and are not added to any marketing list unless you separately opt in.

5. How We Use Information

Information collected through this site is used only to: (a) remember your age-gate confirmation for your current session; (b) send you requested launch updates, if you opted in; and (c) respond to messages submitted through our contact form. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share the limited information described above with third parties, except as required by law or to the extent necessary to operate core site functionality (for example, our website hosting provider). If that changes — for instance, if we adopt a third-party email marketing or CRM platform — this policy will be updated to identify those providers.

7. Data Retention

The age verification cookie is session-based and expires automatically when you close your browser. Waitlist and contact form submissions are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, or until you request deletion.

8. Your Choices & Rights

You may decline the age verification cookie, but doing so means you will not be able to access the site's content, which is restricted to adults 21 and older. You may also request that we delete any waitlist or contact information we hold about you by reaching out using the contact form on our Location page.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is restricted to adults age 21 and older and is not directed to, and does not knowingly collect information from, anyone under 21.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our website and business evolve — particularly as we approach our July 2027 retail launch and add features like e-commerce, analytics, or email marketing. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent through the contact form on our Location page.
