The Cafe

A place to slow down.

Alongside our cannabis storefront, Cafe Canna is building a full coffee bar and lounge — a spot to sit with a book, catch up with a friend, or just take a break, cannabis purchase entirely optional.

Atmosphere

Warm, unhurried, and built for lingering

Think reclaimed wood tables, low light, house plants, and a soundtrack that never competes with conversation. We're designing the cafe as a genuine third place — somewhere to spend an afternoon, not just grab a drink and go.

The cafe operates with its own entrance, counter, and menu, kept operationally separate from cannabis retail in line with Virginia regulations. Coffee, tea, and pastries are available to everyone, regardless of age or cannabis purchase.
